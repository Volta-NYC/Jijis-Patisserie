About

Hello, I'm Manni.

Jiji's Patisserie is Manni Amouah's small batch salt bread project in NYC. It runs on limited Hotplate drops and walk-up pop-ups around Brooklyn.

The bread is Japanese-style shiopan, the same buttery, salty tradition known in Korea as sogeumppang. Jiji's keeps it focused: a few flavors, small runs, and fresh batches that sell until they're gone.
