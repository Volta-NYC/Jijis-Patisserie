## Scrape Limitations & Completeness Notes

1. **Hotplate.com storefront (https://www.hotplate.com/jijispatisserie):** This page is a fully JavaScript-rendered SPA. Standard web fetching returns only meta tags — no menu content, no item details, no event listings. A headless browser would be required to scrape the live storefront. It is also possible that between drops, the storefront shows no menu items at all.

2. **No item-level options, modifiers, or selectors** are visible on any of the provided menus. It is possible that clicking into items on the live Hotplate page reveals options (quantity limits, special instructions) that aren't shown on the Instagram menu graphics.

3. **No item photos** are present on any of the menu graphics — they are text-only designs. Item photos may exist on the Hotplate storefront or on Instagram posts.

4. **The AtOurFriend's menu image was cropped** — additional Pre-Order Menu items may have existed below the visible "Simple Salt Bread Flight."

5. **Menu is not static.** Items, prices, and availability change between pop-up events. The data here reflects two specific snapshots in time.

6. **"AtOurFriend's"** and **"As You Like"** are the names of two different pop-up venue partners. Jiji's Patisserie operates as a traveling pop-up, not a fixed-location bakery.
